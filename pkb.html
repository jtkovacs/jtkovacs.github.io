NAVIGATION

Contents are structured similar to Detlor (2010): MANAGE human and process dimensions; CREATE new data through research or ACQUIRE it elsewhere; ORGANIZE data and STORE it in usable, scalable, secure systems; USE BI software or data science (programming) to ANALYZE data and extract its value; and, finally, DISTRIBUTE information through various media and platforms.

  • MANAGE
  • CREATE & ACQUIRE
  • ORGANIZE & STORE
  • USE & ANALYZE
  • DISTRIBUTE
