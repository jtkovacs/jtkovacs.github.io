Personal Knowledge Base

This is a personal knowledge base in the very broad domain of information management, encompassing activities across the entire information lifecycle, including:

  • The creation of new data through research or its acquisition from vetted sources;
  • The storage and organization of data within usable, scalable, secure systems;
  • The analysis or other use of information to extract its value; and, finally,
  • The distribution of data or information through various media and platforms.

This PKB is an ongoing project, i.e., a mess, and I am its primary audience. I use it to build an overview of how the topics I study interrelate (important for global learners); to summarize and synthesize ideas by posing and answering key questions; and to consolidate references created by myself and others within a single portal.
