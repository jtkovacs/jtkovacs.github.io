1. Study tips

Popova (n.d.) provides a rundown of James Mangan's "ways to acquire knowledge", from his 1936 book You can do anything!:

  • Practice
  • Ask
  • Desire
  • Get it from yourself [reflection]
  • Walk around it [look from multiple angles; don't be narrow-minded]
  • Experiment
  • Teach
  • Read
  • Write
  • Listen
  • Put in order
  • Define
  • Reason
